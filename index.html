AI Research Intern- Stereo Rectification

Iris Technology Inc.

May 2022 - July 2022


↪ Duties: Led a team of undergrad-interns to develop and deploy a simple web app that calibrates a multi-camera for stereo rectification.

➝The task required to use a high resolution RGB camera to view different pallets and compute the coordinate disparity from a TOF camera along with the depth of the coordinates.

➝Currently the disparity is ‘guessed’ manually, whereas our work derived an automated way to compute it using geometry and the intrinsic values like the FOV, focal length, etc.


↪ Achievements: The equations are under review for a research article.Working in a fast paced environment, where minimal direction is required.
