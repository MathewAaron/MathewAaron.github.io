Research Assistant- Controls and Path Planning

N.C. State University

Jan 2022 - May 2022

↪ Advisor: Dr. Edgar Lobaton.

↪ Lab: The Active Robotics Sensing Lab, N.C. State University


↪ Duties: ➝ Led the team responsible for the research and development for the Benchbot with tasks like determining the wheels and motors for the 150 kg robot, deriving the power calculation, and ordering the frames and parts to support the robot, and developing the circuit for the electrical components.

➝ Created a framework of 8 ultrasonic sensors that is currently deployed for localization and an obstacle avoidance system.

➝ Current research includes using a vision based system to integrate SLAM in the system using EKF for localization.

Undergraduate Research Assistant- SLAM

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology

Aug 2019 - May 2020

↪ Advisor: Dr. Shripad Bhatlawande.

↪ Duties: ➝ Developed a robust SLAM based smart cane for assisting visually impaired people localize themselves in an unknown environment.
➝ Designed the cane to accommodate 8 ultrasonic sensors that localizes the user using EKF.

➝ Developed a CNN model to predict staircases from depth images from a mono camera. The model performed 74% accuracy for up-stairs and 56% accuracy for down-stairs.

↪ Achievements: ➝ Presented the project to research faculty from @IITKharagpur and @IITBombay. Published a research paper and presented the finding in a scopus indexed conference..
